Menu




Mocha Frap


Indulge in pure bliss with our Mocha Frappuccino! A rich blend of bold espresso, velvety chocolate, and creamy milk, perfectly blended with ice for a smooth, refreshing treat. Topped with fluffy whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate, every sip is a delightful escape. Cool, creamy and irresistible grab yours now!

Caramel Latte


Savor the smooth, buttery taste of our Caramel Latte! A rich shot of espresso blended with steamed milk and a swirl of golden caramel, creating a perfectly balanced, creamy delight. Finished with a caramel drizzle, each sip is pure indulgence. Warm, sweet, and irresistibly smooth. Treat yourself with one today!

Hazelnut Cappuccino


Warm, nutty, and irresistibly smooth—our Hazelnut Cappuccino is crafted with bold espresso, frothy steamed milk, and a touch of sweet hazelnut syrup. Topped with a delicate layer of foam, it’s a cozy indulgence in every sip. Rich, aromatic, and oh so satisfying treat, try it today at any of our branches.

Vanilla Cold Brew


Smooth, bold, and refreshingly cool—our Vanilla Cold Brew is steeped for hours to perfection, then infused with a touch of creamy vanilla sweetness. Served over ice, it’s the perfect balance of rich coffee flavor and silky smoothness. Cool, bold, and naturally sweet. Fuel your day with every sip!

Irish Cream Latte


A comforting blend of rich espresso and smooth milk infused with velvety Irish cream flavor. Topped with a light foam and a hint of cocoa, it’s your perfect cozy moment in a cup. Indulgent, warm, and deliciously creamy!

Traditional Turkish Coffee


Brewed the authentic way — finely ground beans simmered in a cezve and served unfiltered for a bold, aromatic, and thick experience. Sip slowly, savor the intensity, and taste tradition in every rich cup.

Coconut Iced Coffee


Beat the heat with our tropical twist — chilled espresso, a splash of coconut milk, and a hint of vanilla syrup over ice. Creamy, nutty, and irresistibly refreshing. A beachside vibe in every sip.

Cinnamon Dolce Latte


Sweet and spicy come together in this delightful cup. Espresso mixed with steamed milk and cinnamon dolce syrup, topped with whipped cream and a cinnamon sugar sprinkle. A hug in a mug — cozy and flavorful.
